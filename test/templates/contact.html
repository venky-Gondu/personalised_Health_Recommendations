Technical Stack

Leveraging OpenCV and PIL for image processing, TensorFlow and Keras for machine learning, and NumPy with Pathlib for seamless data manipulation, this system ensures efficient handling of facial images and datasets. The core architecture, based on Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), captures intricate patterns in facial features. Optimized using the Adam optimizer and monitored via TensorBoard, our system delivers precise, timely, and personalized health and diet recommendations based on age estimation.

About This Project

we, RAJESH DUMMA, VENKATESH, VIGNESH independently created this Miniproject for the third semester of our Computer Science Engineering program. We developed a personalized health and diet guidance system using our expertise in image processing and machine learning. Through this project, our goal was to devise a practical and efficient solution for personalized health recommendations based on age estimation, demonstrating our enthusiasm for technology and healthcare.
